Louisiana Beans and Rice

Description

This will give you an inexpensive, yet filling and tasty meal of beans and rice just like those Louisianans do it!

Ingredients

  • Canned kidney beans
  • Andouille sausage
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • White rice
  • Many seasonings

Steps

  1. Cut up the sausage,peppers, onions, and garlic
  2. Cook the peppers, onions, and garlic on high heat until slightly soft
  3. Add seasoning into the vegetables and let cook for another 3 minutes
  4. Add the seasoned vegetables into a large pot with sausage and beans and cook on medium heat
  5. Cook rice
  6. Serve!
