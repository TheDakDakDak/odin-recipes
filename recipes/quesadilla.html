Spicy Cheese Quesadilla

Description

The best thing about this meal is that any idiot can make it!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sliced jalapeno peppers

Steps

  1. Sprinkle cheese onto the quesadilla
  2. Place thinly sliced jalapeno peppers onto the quesadilla
  3. Fold in half and heat in microwave for 40 seconds
  4. Serve!
