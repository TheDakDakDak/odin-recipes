Lasagna

Description

Delicious lasagna that will be so good that you'll never want to eat anything other than lasagna ever again!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Prego tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil 4 of the pasta sheets in salted water
  2. Apply a mixture of ricotta cheese and tomato sauce in between each pasta layer
  3. On the very top, sprinkle mozarella cheese and italian seasoning
  4. Bake lasagna for 1 hour
  5. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top
  6. Serve!
